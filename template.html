Who I am

↩
↩

↩ I am currently an Associate Lecturer in the School of Computer Science↩ and Engineering at the University of New South Wales (UNSW) and a Visiting↩ Database System Scientist at Enmotech Data AU.↩ I obtained my PhD in Computer Science from UNSW under the supervision of Prof. Xuemin Lin (Member of the Academia Europaea, IEEE Fellow) and Prof. Wenjie Zhang (ARC Future Fellow) at the Data and Knowledge Research Group.↩ Prior to that, I received my MEng in Mathematical Computation (First Class Honours) at the Computer Science Department of University College London (UCL), where I was awarded the IBM sponsorship for the↩ Best Undergraduate Final Year Project (First Place).↩ In 2018 and 2021, I worked as an intern at Alibaba DAMO Academy and↩ Google, respectively.↩

Stuff I do

↩
↩

↩ ↩ My expertise lies in developing efficient and scalable algorithms and systems for managing and processing large-scale↩ data, including graph data, relational data, spatial-temporal data, and high-dimensional data, etc.↩ I'm also interested in information retrieval, distributed systems, machine learning, AI meets databases (AI4DB/DB4AI),↩ and AI for social good (AI4SG).↩ ↩
